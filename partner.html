MMM — Movie: Medium of Moments

Partner Program

mmm is a documentary film series that captures fleeting moments of travel, encounter, and light. Each episode is shot on location — roads, coastlines, mountains, cities — with no script, no agenda. Only what's there.

We're looking for brands that share this sensibility. Not advertisers. Fellow travelers with the same eye.

Partnership Tiers

Tier 01

Travel Partner

Your brand travels with us in the credits.

  • Logo in end credits
  • Brand presence on mmm website and social channels
  • Use of film content at events (excluding public release)

From ¥100,000

Tier 02

Journey Partner

Your brand becomes part of the journey.

  • All Travel Partner rights
  • Unrestricted use of the completed film
  • "In partnership with" credit at episode open
  • Appearance of your brand or product within the film
  • Invitation to screenings and preview events

From ¥200,000

A note on integration

Forced placement is not something we do. If your brand or product belongs in the world of mmm — a car on an empty road, a jacket in mountain wind, the key object in a scene — it will appear because it fits. That inevitability is what burns it into the viewer's memory.
